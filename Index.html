STUDYING FOR WAEC

Here are Three apps to help in your study and preparation for your waec exams

myschool.ng

This app is loaded with past questions and will allow you study from wherever you are, with or without an internet connection.

 Ultimatepass

This Is a simple to use and comprehensive mobile app for studying for your WAEC and BECE exams. It covers past questions and detailed answers, making your chances of excellence a lot higher!

Examina

Examina is a very educative, student friendly app that makes studying easier and fun. It has great summaries and explanations, and it enables you to focus well and study at your best capacity
